My Experience: Introduction to Chess

I had gotten to know chess completely by accident. I had gotten a chessboard at a friend's birthday party. It seemed like a fun game and I wanted to learn. So, my parents gave me some time to watch some videos on YouTube to understand chess better.

I really started getting into it. I used to play with my dad, and it would be a 50-50 competition. But on my trip to India, I noticed something in a local bookstore.

It was a chess book. I bought it and took it home. It was this book alone that gave me a base to mold on my chess. Now, I would beat my dad every single time.

My Experience: Building up - Chess

My parents started noticing that I wasn't getting any new knowledge on chess. So, they took me the a chess club. They were the ones that made my base solid, and to build the foundation.

That was also the same time that I had gotten my FIDE rating.

At first, I really enjoyed it. It was so much fun to play like this. However, soon, I stopped making progress.

My Experience: Pros - Chess

I was quite good for my age. So my parents decided to hand me over to professional training. I have been going quite recently every Friday.
